Kurilpa Bridge



Tank Street, Brisbane, Queensland 4000

Previous Next

The bridge stretches from the North Quay end of Tank St in the city to Kurilpa Point in South Brisbane, adjacent to the Queensland Gallery of Modern Art. This is where the CBD and Milton reaches of the river meet.

The $63.3 million bridge completes a pedestrian and bicycle loop linking the city and South Bank via the Goodwill Bridge.

The structure is part of the Brisbane City Centre Master Plan 2006 prepared by the Brisbane City Council and the Queensland Government's South East Queensland Infrastructure Plan and Program 2008-2026.

The project was completed in October 2009, in time for Queensland's 150th-anniversary celebrations.
