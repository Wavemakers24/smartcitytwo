Noosa Chocolate Factory



144 Adelaide Street, Brisbane, Queensland 4000

Previous Next

You may have noticed Noosa Chocolate Factory's mountains of lush chocolate in market stalls throughout Brisbane, but now you don't have to wait until market day to satisfy your sweet tooth.

The permanent home in Adelaide Street features the brand's signature product, fresh Rocky Road, with the softest marshmallows, made fresh daily with strawberry pectin jelly created from scratch.

You can also find Noosa Chocolate Factory's popular chocolate-coated Australian macadamias, almonds and exotic dried fruits. These are available freshly coated in a premium 38 per cent cocoa milk chocolate and 76 per cent cocoa dark chocolate, which also come in buttons.

All products are made locally and free of peanut and palm oils, and there's an extensive gluten-free and dairy-free dark-chocolate range.
